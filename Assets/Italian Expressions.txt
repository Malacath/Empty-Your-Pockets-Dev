Maremma maiala! --> funny Tuscan exclamation. Translated means "Whore of Maremma". Maremma is a region of tuscany and the expression "maremma maiala" is a polite way to swear... Is used in frustrating moments or to emphasize something... Like "maremma maiala, che spavento!" (Maremma maiala, what a fright!)

Che bischero --> funny Tuscan (near Florence) expression. "What a stupid". Bischero means a stupid person, an idiot or a jerk.

Che è 'sto schifo? --> "what is this disgusting thing?" 

Puzza da far schifo --> "It stinks so much". Literally translated is "to stink to disgust."

Questa è sfiga --> "That's bad luck"

Roba da matti. --> "That's crazy." Literally translated is "Stuff for mad people"

Mortacci tua! / Mortacci sua! --> rude Roman phrase. It's an insult that means "your ugly ancestors!" / "His ugly ancestors!"

Vieni qui! --> "Come here!"

Porca miseria. --> Used in sad / frustrating / angry moments. Translated is "pig of misery"

Porco cane --> same of above. Translated is "Dog pig"

Mi hai rotto! / Mi hai rotto le scatole! --> Used when something/someone is annoying you. Literally translated is "You broke me!" / "You broke my boxes!"

E a che diavolo dovrebbe servire?? --> "What the hell am I supposed to do with this?"

Sant'iddio! --> "Holy God!"

Corri! --> "Run!"

Che male! --> "It hurts!"

Madonna santa! / Madonna santissima! --> "Holy Virgin Mary!" / "Very holy Virgin Mary!"

Oddio! --> "Oh my God!"

Gesù Cristo! --> "Jesus Christ!"

Così non va. --> "That's not the way"

Ooh il mio stomaco! --> "Ooh my stomach!"

Ma che è? / Ma che è 'sto coso? --> "What is this?" / "What is this thing?" 

Brutto figlio di... --> "Ugly son of a..."

Figlio d'un cane! --> "son of a dog!"

Mannaggia! --> polite way to say "damn this!"

Accidenti a te! --> "Curse on you!"

Maledizione! --> "Curses!"

Forza! --> "Don't give up!" literally translates means "strenght"

Boh... --> not translatable. Is used to say "I don't know" / "I don't know what's going on"

Che schifo! --> "Disgusting!"

Che puzzo! --> "it stinks!

Sono un genio. --> "I'm a genius"

Muoio di fame --> "I'm starving"

É quasi pronto --> "It's almost ready / done"

Attento! --> "Careful!"

Aiuto! --> "Help!"

Odio queste cose. --> "I hate those things"

Non vedo un tubo! --> "I can't see!". Literally translated is "I can't see a pipe!"

...Come il cavolo a merenda. --> Funny expression that descrives something out of place. Literally translated is "Like a cabbage for snack"

Mi piace un sacco! --> literally translated is "I like it a bag!" but means "I like it much"

Che culo! --> rude way to say "That's luck!". Literally translated is "What an ass!"

In bocca al lupo. --> used to wish luck at someone. Literally translated is "Go inside the wolf's mouth". The other person should answer "crepi" that means "Hoping he will die (the wolf)"

C’è mancato un pelo! --> "That was close!". Literally translated is "We missed an hair"
